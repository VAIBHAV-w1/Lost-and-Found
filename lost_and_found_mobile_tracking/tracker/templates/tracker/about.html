Our Mission

Connecting people with their lost belongings through community, technology, and real-time location intelligence.

Who We Are

The Lost and Found platform was born out of a simple need: a more efficient way to track and recover missing items. We believe that by leveraging modern web technologies like geospatial mapping and real-time messaging, we can significantly increase the chances of happy reunions.

How It Works

Our system uses precise location pinning and distance-based matching algorithms to notify users of potential matches in their vicinity. Whether it's a smartphone left at a cafe or a set of keys found in the park, our platform bridge the gap between finders and owners.
